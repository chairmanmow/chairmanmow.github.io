Harmonic Hands Support

Basic Operation by Page (Horizontal Tab)

  1. Starting and stopping playback

    Tap on the bunny to start playback and the waveform to stop. Use the crown to adjust volume.

  2. Adjusting Synth Parameters

    Control the parameters of two separate oscillators to blend together to generate your unique sound.

  3. Tuning Settings

    Turn on or off autotune and/or adjust the frequency range to control what pitches the synthesizer will generate.

  4. Motion Parameters

    Adjust parameters related to motion in order to adapt to your playing style.

Tips and Tricks

  1. Prevent the app from going to sleep while rotating wrist:

    Adjust WatchOS Setting "Return To Clock":

    Change the return to Clock setting to either "2 minutes" or "1 hour" otherwise the app will sleep when your wrist reaches a certain orientation.

  2. Turn volume low before starting app

    Prevent unwanted volume and protect hearing by rotating the crown downwards on the first view so the volume starts at a low level.

Known issues

  1. Sound may cut out

    This shouldn't happen often but if it does, the app should restart the sound engine if it does. We are investigating this to see if it can be improved

  2. Battery life optimization

    Because this app uses a bit of resources when it is active, your battery will drain quicker than just leaving your watch in and idle state. We are investigating whether it makes sense to try to add in a battery optimized mode, by allowing certain features to be toggled off.
